Mgr.Katarína Gáborová - qualified English and Slovak teacher

I have been working as a freelance language teacher since 2008. My teaching is based on the communicative approach and I use the best of current methodology in combination with innovative features of modern technologies. Lessons are provided in an online form or face-to-face meetings.

In my career I have cooperated with students of all CEFR language levels. I have been leading and supporting company courses, courses in language schools for children, teens and adults, individual courses.
